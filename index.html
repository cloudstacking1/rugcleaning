Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning at least once every 12 to 18 months. However, homes with pets, children, or allergy sufferers may benefit from cleaning every 6 to 12 months. Regular professional cleaning not only improves the appearance of your carpets but also extends their lifespan and maintains a healthier indoor environment.

Is your carpet cleaning service safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. At Rug Cleaning Brooklyn, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are completely safe for children, pets, and sensitive individuals. Our solutions are biodegradable and leave no harmful residue behind. You can have peace of mind knowing your family is protected while still getting a powerful, deep clean.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?

Drying time typically ranges from 2 to 6 hours, depending on the carpet type, thickness, and ventilation in your space. We use high-powered extraction equipment that removes the majority of moisture during the cleaning process, significantly reducing drying time. Opening windows or running fans can help speed up the process further.
